Business Goal Elements

Model / ArchiMate 3.1 Project / Business Goals / Business Goal Elements

Project:
  • GL0 Achieve Real Zero emissions by 2030.
  • GL1 Achieve full decarbonisation of the rail operation by 2030, in line with corporate sustainability targets.
  • GL10 Mitigate environmental and heritage impacts, particularly concerning indigenous heritage areas.
  • GL11 Ensure the project footprint remains within the Snap Back Boundary.
  • GL2 Maintain 192 Mtpa of annual tonnage as a baseline.
  • GL3 Reduce high-potential events related to conflicting train movements and propelling operations while enhancing broader safety measures to meet world-class standards.
  • GL4 Explore opportunities to commercialise the decarbonised rail solution, aiming to bring on Customer Number Two.
  • GL5 Develop a fully integrated system that meets operational outcomes in collaboration with user requirements (to be refined as part of the basis of design process).
  • GL6 Enhance the reliability and availability of the rail network and fleet through optimised maintenance, reduced downtime, and improved system resilience to ensure consistent performance.
  • GL7 Ensure operational simplicity to facilitate the delivery of world-leading performance.
  • GL8 Leverage leading-edge technologies and systems to improve energy efficiency and create long-term value.
  • GL9 Drive to improve cycle times and track access, ensuring operational efficiency without introducing additional bottlenecks or exacerbating existing constraints.
